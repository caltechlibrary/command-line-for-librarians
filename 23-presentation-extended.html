Getting started with ORCID

That was allot of work, why did we do that?

The point of all that was to

  • Know our “Client ID” (application id)
  • Know our “Client Secret” (application private key)

Go to https://orcid.org/developer-tools and in the middle of the page You’ll find a “show details” tab in the section about your “app”. That has the answers we need for “Client ID” and “Client Secret”.
